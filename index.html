Welcome

My name is Carrie. I’m a web developer who specializes in component-based front-end development and has experience creating custom WordPress themes.

Over the past 5 years, I’ve had the opportunity to work on a variety of web-based projects at two agencies. First, at Genuine in Boston, Massachusetts then at GS&F in Nashville, TN.

At Genuine I grew from being a self-taught developer with some digital marketing experience to a confident junior web developer - knocking out bug tickets, styling React components, and coding custom fields for WordPress sites.

At GS&F I worked as an interactive developer, leading projects and getting to use back end and devOps skills - in addition to coding user interfaces.

Both agencies experiences have challenged me and allowed me to grow personally as well as professionally: presenting to clients, balancing multiple deadlines, pushing through impostor syndrome, and remembering to step back and enjoy the ride.
